<--Internet--->

Internet, a system architecture that has revolutionized communications and methods of commerce by allowing various computer networks around the world to interconnect. Sometimes referred to as a “network of networks,” the Internet emerged in the United States in the 1970s but did not become visible to the general public until the early 1990s. By 2020, approximately 4.5 billion people, or more than half of the world's population, were estimated to have access to the Internet

Advantages of Internet

As the Internet is very powerful creation. It has various advantages. It helps us in connecting, communicating and sharing information with one another with the help of it. Students and learners can gather various information and learn various through internet. With the help of internet now we are able to find various locations, contacts and address we need in very certain time period. We can sell various products online through various E-commerce websites and earn money. We are able to pay bills, do banking and shopping by sitting inside the home without going to particular offices or market.

Disadvantages of Internet

Everything in the world have two aspects like that internet has also both advantages and disadvantages. As we have already discussed about advantages now it is a turn of disadvantages. Internet has various disadvantages too. If we spend too much time on internet, we might get addicted. We waste our time in accessing internet for unnecessary things. Internet can lead to people to do various crimes, bullying others etc. Internet makes people addicted to various bad contents like pornography and violent videos.

conclusion

As internet is not bad by itself. It has various advantages which makes our life easier. It has minimized the time occupancy of various works. But over and improper use of anything can harm the human life as like internet does.
