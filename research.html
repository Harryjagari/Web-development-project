Researched by Harendra Jagari

For building this website I have researched from various websites deeply. I have taken the refrence for the basic html structure, but the css and bit of animation and hover effects are totally different from them. Mainly I have used the dealaayo wesite for the product page and then apple, xiaomi, oneplus, samsung,etc. for the photo reference.

1.Navigation bar comparision

I have taken the reference of navigation bar from the vivo's official website here the website has three parts logo on the right and then menu part which is alligned center and the search bar in the left but instead of the searched bar i have used the login button there. The vivo has used the drop down menu in hover effect in the menu buttons, but I have used the normal color changing and drop shadow hover effets. I have also added the redirect link of homepage in the logo. and our navigation bar is sticky in position

2.Product page comparision

I have taken the product page refrence from the nepali websit dealaayo.com. same as it has used the 4 columns to display the mobile products. I have also used the css grid property to make four columns. The animation while hovering the images is also taken from this site, but some changes are made wisely. I have also used the page next buttons. After that the new feature that i have added on my own is the feedback of the customer which is fascinatig for the new visitors in the website. I have also added a image slider in the top of the product page with the cool photos.

3.Image slider comparision

I have taken the refrence of this image slider from the amazon website's home page. For this I have used the javascript where i used the timeinterval property with the slider function. the image slides in every 4 second in my case. And the bonus logic I have used here is the background slider class sets its own size itself respect to the image's size.

4.Login page comparision

I have taken the reference from the netflix login page. I have just taken the outer structure of the form. Additionally, I have added the hover or animation effect in the form. The form gets flipped while changing from login to sign up or sign up to login.

5.Contact Form page comparision

I have taken the refrence of this contact form page from the website called moz. only, the difference here is i have used this form in the left side, whereas the moz website has alligned it vertically center. I have also used the form validation.
